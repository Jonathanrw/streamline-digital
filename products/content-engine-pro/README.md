# Content Engine Pro

**Content Engine Pro** is a high-velocity, entry-level Notion template ($29) designed for creators who need to simplify their content production without complex automation. It focuses on the "Write Once, Distribute Everywhere" philosophy.

## Value Proposition
Save 5+ hours per week on content production by using a structured batching system and platform-specific formatting rules.

## Core Modules
*   **Idea Bank:** Frictionless capture.
*   **365-Day Calendar:** Never wonder what to post again.
*   **Batching Engine:** Moves ideas to formatted posts in 3 simple stages.
*   **Platform Master:** Best practices for LinkedIn, X, and Instagram baked into the system.
*   **Content Pillars:** Strategic topic tracking for brand authority.
*   **Framework Library:** Pre-built writing structures (AIDA, PAS, etc.).

# Affiliate Success Hub

The **Affiliate Success Hub** is a specialized Notion template provided to our partners to help them maximize their commissions and promote **Streamline Digital** products effectively.

## Purpose
To reduce the friction of promotion for our partners by providing all the copy, strategy, and assets they need in one centralized place.

## Core Modules
1.  **[Swipe File Library](./specs/swipe-files.md)**: Structured database of email, social, and newsletter copy.
2.  **[Promotion Calendar](./specs/promo-calendar.md)**: Planning tool for partner launches.
3.  **[Link & Metric Tracker](./specs/links-metrics.md)**: Centralized link management and ROI tracking.
4.  **Brand Asset Kit**: Access to all logos, mockups, and visual assets.

## Why this works
By giving our partners a "system" rather than just a link, we increase their likelihood of promoting us consistently and professionally, leading to higher conversion rates for both parties.

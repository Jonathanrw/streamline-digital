# Drop #13: Client Portal v2 - User Guide

## Introduction
The Client Portal v2 is your centralized hub for managing client relationships and project delivery. This guide will walk you through setting up and using the portal effectively.

## Step 1: Setting up the Notion Workspace
1. Create a new page in your Notion workspace.
2. Duplicate the **Project Timeline**, **Deliverables & Approvals**, and **Communications Log** databases (schema details in `specs/portal-specs.md`).
3. Set up the **Main Portal Template** as the primary landing page for your client.

## Step 2: Customizing for a New Client
1. Duplicate the "Main Portal Template" for each new project.
2. Define the project phases in the **Project Timeline** database.
3. Link the databases to the specific client in your CRM.

## Step 3: Managing Deliverables
1. Add new deliverables to the **Deliverables & Approvals** database.
2. Set due dates and upload files (or links to files).
3. Notify the client when a deliverable is ready for review.

## Step 4: Client Interaction
1. Share the portal with your client using Notion's "Share" feature (Invite them as a guest with "View" or "Comment" access).
2. Clients can provide feedback directly on deliverables and track the project progress in real-time.

## Best Practices
- **Weekly Updates**: Update the Project Timeline once a week to maintain transparency.
- **Centralized Assets**: Always upload final files to the Secure Asset Hub to avoid searching through email threads.
- **Meeting Notes**: Log all meeting decisions in the Communications Log immediately after the call.

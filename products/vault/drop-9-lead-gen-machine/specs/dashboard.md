# Advanced Lead Gen Dashboard Spec

## Visual Components

### 1. The Vital Signs (Callout Blocks)
- **Total Pipeline Value**: Sum of `Estimated Price` from Related Services.
- **Conversion Rate**: (Won / Total Inquiries) * 100.
- **Hot Lead Count**: Count of Tier 1 leads.

### 2. The Command Center (Board View)
- **Groups**: Lifecycle Status (New -> Qualified -> Proposal -> Closing).
- **Sub-group**: Tier (to prioritize the high-score leads within columns).

### 3. Campaign Performance
- **Visual**: A gallery of active Lead Magnets/Campaigns with a rollup showing how many inquiries each generated.

### 4. Nurture Queue
- **View**: List of Tier 2 leads who haven't been contacted in > 7 days.
- **Goal**: Zero out this list daily.

## Aesthetic Requirements
- Dark mode optimized with high-contrast status icons.
- Use of "Progress Bars" for campaign goals.

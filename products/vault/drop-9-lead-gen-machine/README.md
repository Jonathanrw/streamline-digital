This directory contains the full technical specifications for the **Strategy Drop #9: The Advanced Lead Generation Machine**.

## Components
1. **[Inquiries Database Specification (v2)](./specs/inquiries-db.md)**: Multi-channel property list, weighted scoring formula, and lifecycle tracking.
2. **[Service Catalog Database](./specs/service-catalog.md)**: High-ticket service structure and auto-suggestion logic.
3. **[Conversion Dashboard](./specs/dashboard.md)**: Performance visualization and follow-up command center.

## Key Feature: Weighted Multi-Factor Scoring
This advanced version moves beyond simple addition to weighted scoring:
1.  **Budget Weight (50%)**: Direct correlation to revenue potential.
2.  **Timeline Weight (20%)**: Speed of fulfillment.
3.  **Strategic Fit (30%)**: Alignment with our specific niche and "Signature Service."

**Dynamic Categorization:**
-   **🔥 Tier 1: Immediate Booking (80+ pts)**: High budget, immediate need, perfect fit.
-   **🌱 Tier 2: Sequence Nurture (50-79 pts)**: Good fit, long-term potential.
-   **🛡️ Tier 3: Resource Gate (20-49 pts)**: Potential future lead, currently lower budget.
-   **❌ Tier 4: Disqualified (<20 pts)**: Explicit non-fit.

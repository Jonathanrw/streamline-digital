# Paid Growth Blueprint - User Guide

Welcome to the Paid Growth Blueprint. This system is designed to turn your advertising from a "guessing game" into a "math problem." By tracking your data and testing your creative systematically, you can scale your revenue with confidence.

## 1. Filling Your Ad Creative Bank
The best ads feel native and address a specific pain point.
- **Hook Discovery:** Dump 10 different hooks for your primary offer into the `Creative Library`.
- **Winning Indicators:** Once an ad has a CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 1.5% on Meta or > 1% on LinkedIn, tag it as a `Winner`.
- **Repurpose Success:** Take your winning hooks and try them with different visual formats (e.g., turn a winning static image hook into a short video script).

## 2. Setting Up the ROI Calculator
Don't scale what you can't measure.
- **Daily Reconcile:** Spend 5 minutes every morning inputting your previous day's `Spend`, `Leads`, and `Sales`.
- **Know Your Numbers:** The `ROAS` and `CAC` formulas will update automatically. 
- **Scale Triggers:** If your `ROAS` is above 3.0 for 7 days straight, increase your daily budget by 10-15%.

## 3. Mapping Your Targeting Matrix
Reach the right people at the right stage of the funnel.
- **The Cold Layer:** Use "Interest-based" targeting for people who haven't heard of you.
- **The Retargeting Layer:** Always have an audience segment for "Website Visitors (Last 30 Days)" to show them social proof or "Last Chance" offers.
- **Offer Match:** Link each segment to the specific product you are promoting to ensure messaging consistency.

## 4. Keeping the Optimization Log
The difference between a "lucky" marketer and a "successful" one is documentation.
- **Log Every Change:** Whenever you kill an ad or raise a budget, create a new entry in the `Optimization Journal`.
- **The "Monday Audit":** Review your journal every Monday. Look for patterns in why certain ads failed or why a particular audience segment is suddenly getting more expensive.

## Pro Tip: The Blitz Test
Before committing a large budget, run a "Blitz Test": $5/day across 5 different creative variations for 48 hours. The winner of the Blitz Test gets the full campaign budget.

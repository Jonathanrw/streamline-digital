# Vault Drop #10: Operations Wiki & SOP Hub

## Overview
The Operations Wiki & SOP Hub is the "brain" of a scaling business. It centralizes all internal knowledge, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and team onboarding materials into a single, navigable source of truth.

## Value Proposition
As solopreneurs hire their first contractors or transition to small agencies, the biggest bottleneck is repetitive training and fragmented information. This hub saves 5-10 hours a week by:
- Eliminating "Where do I find this?" questions.
- Standardizing service delivery for consistent quality.
- Accelerating team onboarding from days to hours.

## Core Modules
1. **The Knowledge Base**: A structured database for articles, tutorials, and policy documents.
2. **SOP Library**: Step-by-step instructions for recurring tasks (e.g., "How to onboard a client," "Monthly bookkeeping process").
3. **Team Onboarding Portal**: A dedicated workspace for new hires to get up to speed.
4. **Tech Stack Directory**: Centralized management of software tools, logins (via secure links), and billing.

## Target Customer
- Solopreneurs hiring their first virtual assistant (VA).
- Small agency owners (2-10 members) looking to standardize operations.
- Freelancers building a "sellable" asset.

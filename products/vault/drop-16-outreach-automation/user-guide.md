# Outreach Automation Hub - User Guide

Welcome to the Outreach Automation Hub. This system is designed to turn your cold outreach into a predictable, automated machine. Follow this guide to build your authority and book more meetings.

## 1. Setting Up Your Lead Tracker
The Tracker is where you manage the entire lifecycle of a lead.
- **Import Leads:** Use the CSV import tool to add leads from LinkedIn, Apollo, or your own research.
- **Set Lead Scores:** Assign a `Fit Score` and `Value Score` to every new lead. The system will automatically calculate the priority score.
- **Status Management:** Be disciplined about moving leads through the `Status` property. This triggers the correct follow-up reminders.

## 2. Using the Follow-up Sequence Templates
Consistency is key to conversion.
- **Select a Sequence:** Choose between the "Cold Email Authority" or "LinkedIn Relationship" sequences.
- **Personalize the Snippets:** Replace the bracketed text `[Company Name]`, `[Specific Detail]` with researched info to maintain a human touch.
- **Follow the Delay:** The `Delay` property tells you exactly when to send the next touch. Check your `Daily Outreach Queue` every morning.

## 3. The Lead Scoring System
Work on the right leads at the right time.
- **Priority Filter:** Always start your day with the `High Value Leads` view. These are the leads most likely to convert and provide the highest ROI.
- **Dynamic Updates:** As leads reply or engage, update their `Engagement Score` to see their priority rise in real-time.

## 4. Analyzing Your Conversion Dashboard
Data tells the truth that feelings ignore.
- **Review Response Rates:** If your response rate is < 5%, it's time to audit your subject lines and "Initial Touch" script.
- **Audit Follow-ups:** Look at the "Average Follow-ups" metric. If you're booking most meetings on touch #4, make sure you're not giving up after touch #2.

## Pro Tip: The Daily Outreach Ritual
Spend the first 45 minutes of your day in the `Daily Outreach Queue`. Send your new initiations first, then knock out all pending follow-ups. Consistency in the first hour of your day creates predictable revenue in the last week of your month.

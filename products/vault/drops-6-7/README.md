# The Vault: Strategy Drops #6 & #7

This directory contains the specifications and mockups for the next evolution of The Vault subscription.

## Drop #6: Advanced CRM & Lifecycle Tracker
A high-fidelity relationship management system designed for scaling freelancers.
- **Goal**: Move from transactional work to relationship-based business growth.
- **Key Features**: Health scoring, automated touchpoint reminders, and a referral partner database.

## Drop #7: Product Launch OS
The definitive blueprint for planning and executing digital product launches.
- **Goal**: Eliminate the chaos of product launches through standardized systems.
- **Key Features**: Copy library, asset production trackers, and a 14-day execution calendar.

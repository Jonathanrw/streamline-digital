# Drop #12: The Email Marketing Engine

Standardize your email marketing with high-converting sequence templates and automation logic.

## Summary
The Email Marketing Engine moves you from "randomly sending emails" to a systematic, automated conversion machine. It focuses on the four critical pillars of email marketing: Onboarding (Welcome), Recovery (Abandoned Cart), Engagement (Newsletter), and Delivery (Lead Magnets).

## Contents
- **Strategy & Philosophy**: Core modules and the 5-part Indoctrination sequence.
- **Technical Specs**: Sequence logic, triggers, and tagging protocol.
- **Copy Templates**: Fill-in-the-blank templates for Welcome and Abandoned Cart sequences.
- **Dashboard Command Center**: HTML/CSS interactive dashboard for tracking metrics.

## Files
- `strategy.md` - High-level overview and module definitions.
- `specs/automation-specs.md` - Technical logic and segmentation rules.
- `copy-templates.md` - Email swipe files.
- `dashboard.html` - Interactive command center mockup.
- `dashboard-preview.png` - Visual preview of the dashboard.

## Value Proposition
Turn cold traffic into warm leads and repeat customers on autopilot without the manual effort of drafting every campaign from scratch.

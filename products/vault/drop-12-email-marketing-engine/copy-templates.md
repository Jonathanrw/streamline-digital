# Email Marketing Engine: Copywriting Templates

## 1. Indoctrination (Welcome) Sequence

### Email 1: The Delivery
**Subject**: [Welcome] Here is your [Lead Magnet Name]
**Body**:
Hey [First Name],

I'm so glad you're here. As promised, here is the link to download the [Lead Magnet Name]:

[Link]

This is just the first step in [Achieving Result]. Over the next few days, I'm going to share some of the most effective strategies I've learned for [Problem Area].

To make sure you don't miss anything, please drag this email to your "Primary" tab if you're using Gmail.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]

---

### Email 2: The Origin Story
**Subject**: How I went from [Struggle] to [Success]
**Body**:
[First Name], I wasn't always the [Current Role] I am today.

[Short story about your "Aha!" moment or the struggle you faced].

That's why I built [Your Business/Product]. I wanted to make sure nobody else had to [Struggle].

Tomorrow, I'll share a quick win you can implement in under 10 minutes.

Best,
[Your Name]

---

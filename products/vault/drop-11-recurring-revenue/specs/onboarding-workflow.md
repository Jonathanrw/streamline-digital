# Technical Specs: Recurring Service Onboarding Workflow

## Phase 1: The Transition (Pre-Onboarding)
1. **Contract Execution**: Send the Retainer Agreement (standardized template).
2. **First Payment**: Setup recurring billing (LemonSqueezy/Stripe).
3. **Data Migration**: Collect any existing logins/assets from previous one-off projects.

## Phase 2: Kickoff
1. **Recurring Roadmap Session**: 45-minute call to define goals for the next 3-6 months.
2. **Communication Setup**: Slack channel, dedicated email, or shared Notion portal.
3. **Monthly Reporting Cycle**: Confirm the date and format for performance reports.

## Phase 3: Implementation
1. **Baseline Audit**: Final check of current systems before recurring work starts.
2. **First Month Deliverables**: High-impact "quick wins" to build momentum.
3. **Check-in #1**: Formal check-in after week 2 to ensure expectations are met.

## Database: Onboarding Checklist
- **Task**: Title
- **Stage**: Select (Transition, Kickoff, Implementation)
- **Assigned To**: Person
- **Status**: Checkbox
- **Related Client**: Relation (to Retainer Clients)

# Vault Drop #11: Recurring Revenue Blueprint

## Overview
The Recurring Revenue Blueprint is designed to help solopreneurs and freelancers stabilize their income by transitioning from the "feast-or-famine" project cycle to predictable, monthly recurring revenue (MRR).

## Value Proposition
Turn one-off clients into long-term partners with standardized retainer packages and productized services, saving hours on sales and administrative overhead while increasing customer lifetime value (LTV).

## Core Components
1. **Retainer Package Designer**: A framework for defining what goes into a monthly retainer.
2. **Standard Retainer Agreement (Template)**: Legal-ready language for recurring service contracts.
3. **Recurring Service Onboarding Workflow**: A specialized onboarding process for long-term clients.
4. **Client Health & Retention Dashboard**: A Notion system for tracking retainer status, deliverables, and client satisfaction.
5. **Monthly Performance Report Template**: A standardized report to demonstrate value to clients every month.

## Target Customer
- Freelancers who are tired of chasing new projects every month.
- Digital creators offering maintenance or ongoing support.
- Solo-agencies looking to productize their high-demand services.

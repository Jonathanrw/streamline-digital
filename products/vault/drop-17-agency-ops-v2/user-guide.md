# Agency Operations v2 - User Guide

Welcome to Agency Operations v2. This drop is designed to help you cross the chasm from solopreneurship to agency ownership. By implementing these systems, you shift the burden of delivery from yourself to a high-performing, incentivized team.

## 1. Setting Up the Multi-User SOP Engine
Knowledge is the only asset that doesn't depreciate if documented correctly.
- **Migrate Your SOPs:** Move your existing single-user SOPs into the new Hub.
- **Assign Roles:** Use the `Role Assigned` property to ensure team members only see what's relevant to them.
- **Verification Cycle:** Set the `Last Verified` date. Assign "Verification Owners" to keep the documentation alive without your constant intervention.

## 2. Launching the Team Performance Dashboard
You can't manage what you don't measure.
- **Invite Your Team:** Add your contractors or employees to the `Team Members` database.
- **Time Logging Habit:** Require the team to log their minutes daily. This is the only way the `Utilization %` and `Capacity Planner` formulas work.
- **The Weekly Utilization Review:** Check your Dashboard every Friday. If someone is consistently at 90%+, it's time to hire or adjust their scope.

## 3. Implementing Profit-Sharing Models
Turn employees into "Intrapreneurs."
- **Choose Your Share %:** Start conservative (e.g., 5-10% of gross profit).
- **Transparency:** Use the `Profit Pools` database to show the team exactly how their performance affects the shared pool.
- **Payout Schedule:** We recommend quarterly payouts to align long-term stability with short-term incentives.

## 4. Using the Capacity Planner to Forecast
Stop the "Hire in a Panic" cycle.
- **Map Active Hours:** The system automatically sums hours from `Active Projects`.
- **The Traffic Light Rule:** 
  - **Green (0-70%):** You have room to sell.
  - **Yellow (71-85%):** Start looking for talent. Post a job ad.
  - **Red (86%+):** You are at risk of burnout and quality drop. Do not take on new work until capacity is added.

## Pro Tip: The Monthly Operations Audit
Once a month, spend 30 minutes reviewing the `Verification Queue` and `Individual Payouts`. Use this data during your 1-on-1s to provide objective feedback and clear growth paths for your team members.

# Agency Operations v2 - Strategic Blueprint

## Value Proposition
The transition from solopreneur to agency owner is the most dangerous phase of business growth. Agency Operations v2 provides the structural integrity needed to scale from "doing the work" to "owning the system." This drop focuses on multi-user coordination, performance accountability, and sustainable profit-sharing models that align team incentives with business growth.

## Core Modules
1. **Team Performance Dashboard:** Real-time visibility into contractor/employee utilization, billable vs. non-billable hours, and project delivery speed.
2. **Advanced SOP Multi-User Engine:** A permission-based standard operating procedure hub designed for collaborative updates and role-specific views.
3. **Profit-Sharing & Incentive Models:** Financial templates for implementing "Performance-Based Pay" and "Profit Pools" to reduce turnover and increase owner-independence.
4. **Capacity Planner:** A forecasting tool to determine when to hire next based on current utilization and sales pipeline.

## Success Metrics
- **Owner Freed Hours:** Reducing the owner's involvement in delivery by at least 15 hours/week within 60 days.
- **Team Utilization:** Maintaining an average 70-80% utilization rate across the agency.
- **Profit Margin Stability:** Ensuring a 20%+ net profit margin even with increased payroll.

## Implementation Roadmap
- **Week 1: Inventory.** Map out all current delivery tasks and assign "Role" tags.
- **Week 2: SOP Migration.** Transition single-user SOPs into the Multi-User Engine.
- **Week 3: Incentive Setup.** Implement the Profit-Sharing model for the first core team member.
- **Week 4: Dashboard Launch.** Set up the Team Performance Dashboard and hold the first "Metrics Review" meeting.

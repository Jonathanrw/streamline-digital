# The 'Admin Drag' Diagnostic Tool
## Identifying Your Operational Bottlenecks

Welcome to the Vault. This audit is designed to help you pinpoint exactly where you are losing time and energy in your business. By the end of this test, you will know which Vault Strategy Drops to prioritize to reclaim your 10+ hours per week.

### Section 1: The Scorecard
Rate yourself on a scale of 1-5 for each statement (1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree).

1. **Information Retrieval**: "I can find any client contract, invoice, or meeting note in under 30 seconds."
2. **Repetitive Tasks**: "I spend less than 2 hours a week on manual data entry or copying information between tools."
3. **Process Clarity**: "If I hired a virtual assistant today, I have written SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) ready for 80% of my core tasks."
4. **Lead Management**: "Every new inquiry is automatically captured and I have a clear, immediate follow-up sequence."
5. **Content Distribution**: "I create content once and it is systematically distributed across multiple platforms without me manually posting everywhere."
6. **Financial Visibility**: "I know my exact profit margin and real hourly rate for this month without opening a spreadsheet."
7. **Client Onboarding**: "From the moment a client pays, their project is set up and they receive a welcome kit automatically."

---

### Section 2: Calculating Your 'Admin Drag'
Add up your scores from the 7 questions above:

*   **Score 7-15: High Drag (The Bottleneck Phase)**
    You are drowning in manual work. Your business depends entirely on your presence, and scaling feels impossible because you're already at capacity.
    *   **Your Priority**: Drop #2 (Automation Audit) & Drop #10 (Operations Wiki).
*   **Score 16-25: Moderate Drag (The Growth Mess Phase)**
    You have some systems, but they are fragmented. You’re scaling, but every new client adds a layer of complexity that stresses your current setup.
    *   **Your Priority**: Drop #3 (Lead Gen Machine) & Drop #5 (Scaling Dashboard).
*   **Score 26-35: Low Drag (The Automated Solopreneur Phase)**
    You are lean and efficient. You have successfully decoupled your time from your delivery. You are ready to scale to a productized or agency model.
    *   **Your Priority**: Drop #11 (Recurring Revenue) & Drop #17 (Agency Operations).

---

### Section 3: The ARC Framework
For every task that scored a 1 or 2, apply the ARC logic:
1.  **Automate**: Can a tool (Zapier/Make/Native Integration) handle this without my input?
2.  **Remove**: Is this task actually driving revenue or client satisfaction? If not, kill it.
3.  **Centralize**: If it must be done manually, is it being done in a central hub (Notion) or scattered across 5 different apps?

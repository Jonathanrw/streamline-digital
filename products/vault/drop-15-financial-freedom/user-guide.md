# Financial Freedom Dashboard - User Guide

Welcome to your Financial Freedom Dashboard. This system is designed to give you absolute clarity over your business finances and your path to long-term wealth. Follow these steps to set up your financial operating system.

## 1. Setting Up the Transactions Hub
Your business runs on data. Let's get it organized.
- **Import History:** Use the Notion "Merge with CSV" feature to import your bank or Stripe transactions from the last 3-6 months.
- **Categorize for Tax:** Go through your expenses and assign a `Category`. This will automatically populate your P&L and Tax Calculator.
- **Recurring vs. One-Time:** Mark recurring software costs as `Subscriptions` to ensure they show up in your Subscription Audit.

## 2. Managing Your Tax Reserves
Never be surprised by a tax bill again.
- **Set Your Rate:** Edit the `Tax Rate` formula in the `Tax Reserves` database to match your local income tax rate.
- **Track Your Savings:** Every time you move money to a separate tax savings account, record the amount in the `Amount Saved` property.
- **The met/Under Status:** Aim to keep your status at `Met` to ensure you're fully covered for your next quarterly payment.

## 3. Planning for Wealth
Your business should fund your life, not just your software subscriptions.
- **Define Your Goals:** Add your primary personal wealth goals (e.g., IRA, Emergency Fund, House Down Payment).
- **Automate Contributions:** Determine a percentage of your monthly profit to allocate to these goals.
- **Monitor Progress:** Use the `Progress Bar` view to stay motivated as you see your "Current Value" climb toward your "Target Value."

## 4. Conducting a Subscription Audit
Stop the "leaky bucket" of unused software.
- **Review Monthly:** Once a month, check the `Cost Optimization` view.
- **Score Your Tools:** Be ruthless. If a tool isn't "Critical," consider cancelling it or switching to a lower tier.
- **Track Annual Savings:** See the "Annual Cost" formula to understand how much a small $20/month subscription actually costs you over the year.

## Pro Tip: The Monthly Close
On the 1st of every month, spend 20 minutes reconciling your accounts in the Transactions Hub. Review your P&L, update your Tax Reserves, and check your Investment goal progress. This one habit is the difference between a "job" and a "business."

# Vault Drop #21: Client Feedback & Referral Loop Automation - User Guide

This system is designed to help you build authority and leverage your existing network for growth.

## 1. Setting Up the Testimonial Library
Your `Testimonial Library` is the source of truth for your social proof.
- **Categorization**: Use tags (e.g., "Speed", "Quality", "Strategy") to filter testimonials for specific sales pitches.
- **Permission Tracking**: Always mark whether you have permission to use the client's name, photo, and company logo.
- **Asset Links**: Link to the original screenshot or video file for high-fidelity proof.

## 2. Automating Feedback Requests
Don't wait for clients to volunteer feedback.
- **The "High-Five" Moment**: Trigger a feedback form immediately after a project milestone is marked "Complete" in your CRM.
- **Structured Questionnaire**: Ask specific questions (e.g., "What was your #1 frustration before working with us?") to generate high-quality copy for your marketing.
- **Follow-up Logic**: Set a reminder to follow up if a request hasn't been answered within 48 hours.

## 3. Launching Your Referral Engine
Turn your clients into your sales team.
- **Partner Onboarding**: Invite your happiest clients to become "Founding Referral Partners."
- **Incentive Structure**: Decide on your reward (e.g., 20% commission or a free month of "The Vault").
- **Payout Tracking**: Use the `Referral Payouts` database to ensure partners are rewarded promptly, building long-term trust.

## 4. Social Media Integration
- **Quote-Card Workflow**: Use the approved templates from the Social Media Asset Kit to turn text testimonials into high-stopping power graphics.
- **Tagging Strategy**: When sharing a testimonial, tag the client to increase reach and social proof.

---
*Pro Tip: Add a "Referral Bonus" link to your email signature and client portal footer for passive lead generation.*

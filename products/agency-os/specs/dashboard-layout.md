# Agency OS: Dashboard Layout Specification

## 1. Top Section: Critical Actions & Alerts
*   **Quick Actions:** Buttons for "New Project," "Add Client," "New Invoice," and "Team Check-in."
*   **Urgent Tasks:** Filtered view showing "Urgent" tasks or tasks due today/overdue.
*   **Accounts Receivable Alert:** Small view showing "Overdue" invoices.

## 2. Main Body: Operational Hub
*   **Left Column (60%): Portfolio Overview**
    *   A gallery or list view of active Projects, showing health icons and progress bars.
    *   Links to the individual Client Portals.
*   **Right Column (40%): Team & Pipeline**
    *   Workload balance view (Team members and their active hours).
    *   Lead status summary (Who is in "Onboarding").

## 3. Bottom Section: Business Health
*   **Finance Snapshot:** Simple metrics showing MRR, Monthly Revenue, and Net Profit.
*   **Resource Library:** Links to the SOP Wiki, Brand Assets, and Team Directory.

## 4. Navigation Sidebar
*   🏡 **Dashboard** (Main)
*   🤝 **Clients** (Database View)
*   📁 **Projects** (Database View)
*   👥 **Team Ops** (Workflow Page)
*   💰 **Finance** (Reporting Page)
*   📚 **SOP Wiki** (Documentation)

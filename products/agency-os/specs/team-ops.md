# Agency OS: Team Operations Specification

## 1. Team Directory Database
Manage team members and their roles.

**Properties:**
*   **Name** (Title)
*   **Role** (Select: Creative Director, Designer, Developer, Copywriter, Account Manager)
*   **Capacity (Hours/Week)** (Number)
*   **Current Load** (Rollup: Sum of Estimated Hours of "In Progress" tasks)
*   **Contact Info** (Email/Slack)
*   **Availability** (Select: Full-time, Part-time, Freelance)

## 2. Team Weekly Review
A structured workflow for aligning the team.

**Components:**
*   **Review Database:** Linked to Team Members. Tracks weekly wins, blockers, and next week's focus.
*   **Automatic Summaries:** Notion filters to show tasks completed in the last 7 days.
*   **Blocker Board:** A view specifically for tasks with the status "Blocked" across all projects.

## 3. Operations Manual (Wiki)
Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for the agency.

**Key SOPs included in template:**
*   How we onboard a client
*   Our design review process
*   How to handle invoicing
*   Brand guidelines for the agency

# Agency Operating System (Agency OS)

The **Agency Operating System** is a premium ($99) Notion template designed to be the central nervous system for small freelance agencies. It moves beyond simple task tracking into full-scale agency management, covering client onboarding, project delivery, team coordination, and advanced financial tracking.

## Core Modules

1.  **[Client Portals](./specs/client-portals.md)**: Manage the full client lifecycle from onboarding to offboarding, with dedicated client-facing hubs.
2.  **[Project Management](./specs/project-management.md)**: Multi-client project tracking with progress bars, health indicators, and team workload balancing.
3.  **[Team Operations](./specs/team-ops.md)**: Manage team capacity, roles, and standard operating procedures (SOPs).
4.  **[Billing & Finance](./specs/billing-finance.md)**: Advanced invoicing, revenue forecasting, and profitability analysis.
5.  **[Dashboard Layout](./specs/dashboard-layout.md)**: A high-fidelity central cockpit for the agency owner.

## Target Audience
- Freelance agencies scaling to 2-10 team members.
- Solopreneurs managing high-volume client work who need a "system" to prevent things from falling through the cracks.
- Small design, marketing, or development shops.

## Key Differentiators
- **Health Indicators:** Automatic project health status based on real data.
- **Capacity Management:** See exactly who is overworked before assigning new tasks.
- **Integrated CRM & Billing:** Linking the financial side directly to project delivery.

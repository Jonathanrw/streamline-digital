# Social Media Asset Kit: Design Specifications

## Overview
This kit provides professional, high-converting social media templates for Solopreneurs to promote their services and the "Solopreneur Workflow Hub" system.

## Design Aesthetic
- **Minimalist Professional**: Clean typography, ample white space.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Indigo (#4F46E5) - Primary Accent
  - Slate Gray (#374151) - Text
  - White (#FFFFFF) - Background
- **Typography**: Sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or Roboto).

## 1. Instagram Kit (Square - 1080x1080)
### Templates:
1. **"The Cost of Chaos"**: A carousel template highlighting common administrative bottlenecks vs. the solution.
2. **"Project Spotlight"**: Showcase a successful client project using the Project Manager database.
3. **"Quick Tip"**: A single-slide template for productivity hacks.

## 2. LinkedIn/Twitter Kit (Landscape - 1200x630 / 1600x900)
### Templates:
1. **"Systems Showcase"**: A wide graphic showing the Workflow Hub dashboard.
2. **"Authority Post"**: Text-heavy graphic with a professional profile photo and a key quote.
3. **"Banner Assets"**: Profile header images with "Streamline Your Workflow" messaging.

## 3. Implementation Guide
- **Software**: Templates are designed for Figma or Canva.
- **Usage**: Replace placeholder text and images with your own branding.
- **Export**: Export as PNG or WebP for optimal quality.

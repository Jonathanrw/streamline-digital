# SOP: Delivering the Workflow Audit

## Phase 1: Pre-Audit Intake
1.  **Kickoff Call**: Schedule a 30-minute kickoff call to establish expectations and access.
2.  **Access Grant**: Ensure the client has access to the **Intake Form**.
3.  **Discovery Collection**: Monitor the **Discovery Log** in the client's dashboard as they fill it out.

## Phase 2: The ARC Audit Process
1.  **Task Inventory**: Categorize every task in the **Task Audit** database.
2.  **ARC Evaluation**:
    *   **A - Automate**: Is there a 10x ROI for automating this?
    *   **R - Remove**: Is this task a "vanity metric" or "legacy process"?
    *   **C - Centralize**: Can this be grouped with similar tasks in a single tool?
3.  **ROI Calculation**: Validate the `Monthly Time Burden` and `Automation ROI` formulas to ensure they match the client's current hourly rate.

## Phase 3: Reporting & Roadmap
1.  **Generate Report**: Use the **Client Audit Report Template** to synthesize findings.
2.  **Phased Roadmap**: Break down implementations into three phases:
    *   **Phase 1: Quick Wins** (Highest ROI, lowest complexity).
    *   **Phase 2: Core Infrastructure** (CRM, Billing, Fulfillment).
    *   **Phase 3: Scaling** (Advanced flows, team management).
3.  **Presentation**: Present the report via recorded Loom or live Zoom session.

## Phase 4: Implementation (Upsell)
1.  **Track Progress**: Transition the client to the **Implementation Tracker**.
2.  **Regular Syncs**: Review the `Workflow Health Score` monthly to demonstrate progress and retain high-ticket clients.

# Workflow Audit & Optimization Dashboard

## Value Proposition
The Workflow Audit & Optimization service is a high-ticket productized offering ($499+) designed to help solopreneurs and small agencies transition from "chaos" to "clockwork." By systematically auditing existing processes through the ARC Framework (Automate, Remove, Centralize), we provide a clear roadmap for scaling without increasing burnout.

## Core Modules
1. **Intake & Discovery**: A comprehensive questionnaire to capture current tech stack, pain points, and time-leaks.
2. **ARC Audit Engine**: A specialized database that categorizes every task and provides an automation ROI calculation.
3. **Customized Scaling Roadmap**: A phased implementation plan with prioritized "quick wins" and long-term systems building.
4. **Implementation Tracker**: A collaborative workspace for tracking the progress of new system deployments.

## Target Customer
- Scaling solopreneurs hitting a "time ceiling."
- Small agency owners struggling with inconsistent service delivery.
- Freelancers looking to productize their operations.

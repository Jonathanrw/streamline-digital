# Module 1: Intake & Discovery

## Purpose
Capture the "Current State" of the client's business to feed the ARC Audit engine.

## Intake Questionnaire (Notion Form or Integrated Typeform)
- **Business Vitals**: Niche, Team Size, Current Revenue, Target Revenue.
- **The Tech Stack**: List of all tools currently paid for (SaaS audit).
- **The Pain Points**:
    - "What task do you dread most every week?"
    - "Where does the ball get dropped most often?"
    - "How many hours a week are you spending on non-billable admin?"
- **Workflow Deep Dive**:
    - Describe your lead-to-client journey.
    - Describe your project delivery process.

## Notion Database: Client Discovery Log
- **Property: Area** (Sales, Delivery, Admin, Marketing).
- **Property: Pain Level** (Select: Low, Medium, High, Critical).
- **Property: Description** (Text).
- **Property: Related ARC Audit Items** (Relation to ARC Database).

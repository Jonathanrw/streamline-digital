# The 10+ Hour Reclaim: The ARC Framework for Business Systems

"I don't have time to build systems." 

This is the most common excuse we hear from solopreneurs. It's also the reason they remain stuck. You don't build systems because you have time; you build systems to **create** time.

At Streamline Digital, we use the **ARC Framework** to help founders reclaim 10+ hours of their week.

## 1. Automate
The first step is identifying repetitive, low-value tasks that can be handled by software. 
*   **Lead Intake**: From inquiry to your CRM without touching a key.
*   **Payment Notifications**: Automated follow-ups for late invoices.
*   **Recurring Reports**: Dashboard updates that populate while you sleep.

If a human has to do it twice a week, a machine should do it forever.

## 2. Remove
Most productivity "hacks" are just more noise. We audit your current tool stack to find the "Busy Work" that doesn't move the needle. 
*   Are you checking 3 different analytics dashboards?
*   Do you have 4 different ways for clients to message you?
*   Are you maintaining a "productivity spreadsheet" that takes more time to update than the work itself?

If it doesn't contribute to revenue or deep work, **Remove it**.

## 3. Consolidate
Context switching is the silent killer of productivity. Moving between 5 disconnected tools causes "Cognitive Friction" that drains your energy.

Consolidate your business into a **Single Source of Truth**. When your lead tracking, project management, and finance live in one Notion OS, the mental load of running a business drops by 80%.

# Scaling Your Agency: Why founders burn out at 3-5 Clients

Most service businesses hit a "ceiling" at 3-5 high-ticket clients. 

At this stage, you're making good money, but your life is a mess. You're the primary project manager, the account executive, and the lead technician. You're not scaling; you're just trading more sleep for more revenue.

## The Delivery Gap
The gap between "signing a client" and "delivering results" is where most agencies fail. If your onboarding process requires a 2-hour manual setup and a dozen back-and-forth emails, you cannot scale.

Professionalism that scales requires **Standardization**.

*   **Timeline Transparency**: Clients know exactly where the project stands without asking you for an update.

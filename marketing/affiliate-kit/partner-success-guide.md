## 📈 How to Drive Sales

### 1. The "Efficiency Audit" Angle
Solopreneurs are overwhelmed. Use the **Success Checklist** (our lead magnet) to show them where they are losing time, then position the **Workflow Hub** as the solution.

### 2. The "Operating a Team" Angle
For those ready to scale, promote **Drop #19 (Collaborative Systems)** or **Drop #24 (Content Agency OS)**. These are high-value systems that save founders from the headache of managing contractors.

### 3. Show, Don't Just Tell
If you use our systems, record a quick 60-second Loom walkthrough of your Notion workspace. Raw, authentic "behind-the-scenes" content consistently converts better than polished ads.

## 🤝 Best Practices & Rules
- **Be Honest**: Share your actual experience with the products.
- **No Spamming**: We value our brand reputation. Please avoid unsolicited DMs or spammy forum posts.
- **Disclose**: Always use a clear affiliate disclosure (e.g., "#ad" or "I may earn a commission if you purchase through this link").

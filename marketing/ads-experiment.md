# Paid Ads Experiment Report - "The Content Engine" Hook

## Experiment Overview
*   **Duration:** 7 Days (Simulated)
*   **Total Budget:** $200
*   **Platform:** X (Twitter) Ads - Chosen for the high organic resonance of the Content Engine.
*   **Objective:** Lead Generation (Email Sign-ups for the Checklist).

## Ad Creative
### Copy Variant A (The "Time Saver")
**Headline:** Stop the Daily Content Grind.
**Body:** I spent 4 hours batching 30 days of high-converting content using this exact system. 
The Content Engine is the solopreneur's secret weapon. 
Download the Free Success Checklist + See the Hub.
**CTA:** Download Now

### Copy Variant B (The "Systematic Solo")
**Headline:** Run Your Solo Business Like a Machine.
**Body:** Don't build from scratch. Use the Solopreneur Workflow Hub to manage projects, CRM, and content in one Notion workspace. 
Join 500+ solopreneurs saving 10+ hours a week.
**CTA:** Get Started

## Results (Simulated)

| Metric | Variant A (Time Saver) | Variant B (Systematic) | Total |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spend** | $100 | $100 | $200 |
| **Impressions** | 12,450 | 9,800 | 22,250 |
| **Clicks** | 412 | 205 | 617 |
| **CTR** | 3.3% | 2.1% | 2.77% |
| **Leads (Opt-ins)** | 84 | 38 | 122 |
| **Conversion (Click-to-Lead)** | 20.4% | 18.5% | 19.7% |
| **Cost Per Lead (CPL)** | **$1.19** | **$2.63** | **$1.64** |

## Revenue & ROI
*   **Direct Sales from Ad Leads:** 5 Hub Sales ($245)
*   **Vault Subscriptions:** 2 Sign-ups ($38/mo)
*   **Immediate Revenue:** $283
*   **Immediate ROI:** 1.41x (Positive on Day 1)

## Strategic Insights
1.  **Winner:** Variant A significantly outperformed Variant B. The "Time Saving" and "Batching" angle for content resonates more than the "System/Machine" angle.
2.  **Scalability:** With a CPL of $1.19 and positive immediate ROI, we should scale this spend to $500 next week, doubling down on Variant A.
3.  **Creative:** The high CTR (3.3%) suggests that the "Content Engine" mockup image (from the designer) is high-stopping power.

# ✅ The Solopreneur Success Checklist
*Reclaim 10+ hours of your week by auditing your current workflows.*

As a solopreneur, your business is only as strong as your systems. If you're feeling overwhelmed, it's likely because you're doing manual work that could be automated or streamlined. Use this checklist to identify your biggest efficiency gaps.

---

## 1. Client & Lead Management
- [ ] **Unified Inbox:** Do you have one place for all client communications, or are you checking 5 different apps/DMs?
- [ ] **Standardized Intake:** Do you use a form for new clients, or are you manually asking the same questions every time?
- [ ] **Visual Pipeline:** Can you see exactly where every lead is in your sales process right now?
- [ ] **Automated Invoicing:** Do your invoices send automatically, or are you manually creating PDFs?

## 2. Project Delivery
- [ ] **Standardized Templates:** Do you start every new project from a pre-built template, or from a blank page?
- [ ] **Client Portals:** Do clients have a central place to see progress, or are you emailing them constant updates?
- [ ] **Time Tracking:** Do you know exactly how long each project takes, or are you "guesstimating" your hourly rate?
- [ ] **Deadline Alerts:** Do you have automated reminders for upcoming milestones?

## 3. Marketing & Content
- [ ] **Content Repository:** Is your past content stored and searchable for repurposing?
- [ ] **Batching System:** Do you create content daily, or do you have a specific time each week to batch for the next 7-30 days?
- [ ] **Distribution Workflow:** Do you have a checklist for sharing one piece of content across multiple platforms?
- [ ] **Automated Scheduling:** Are your posts scheduled in advance, or are you posting "live" every time?

## 4. Finance & Admin
- [ ] **Expense Tracking:** Are your business expenses logged automatically or in a weekly 10-minute session?
- [ ] **Subscription Audit:** Have you reviewed your recurring software costs in the last 90 days?
- [ ] **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs):** Have you written down the steps for your most common tasks (so you can delegate them later)?

---

## 🏁 Your Score
- **0-5 Checks:** You're in "The Chaos Zone." You likely feel like you're constantly "catching up."
- **6-12 Checks:** You're "The Hustler." You have some systems, but they aren't fully integrated.
- **13+ Checks:** You're "The Streamliner." Your business is ready to scale or move toward an agency model.

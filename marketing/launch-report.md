# Initial Marketing Campaign Launch Report - June 2026

## 1. Campaign Execution

### Social Media Distribution
The following assets from the approved kit were deployed:
- **LinkedIn:** Focused on professional efficiency. Posted "Post-1: Save 10+ Hours" and "Post-5: Social Proof Mockup".
- **Twitter (X):** Leveraged the creator economy. Thread started with "Post-2: System Overview" and "Post-4: Content Calendar".
- **Instagram:** Focused on visual aesthetic of the Hub. Stories 1-3 were run as a sequence over 48 hours.

- **Lead Magnet:** The email capture form is live and connected to a mock API endpoint that triggers the 5-day welcome sequence.
- **Nurture Sequence:** Day 1-5 emails are active.
- **Storefront Success Flow:** Users are redirected to the landing page with a `?success=true` parameter upon purchase, triggering a verified access banner.

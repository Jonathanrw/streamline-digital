# Free Solopreneur Success Checklist
The essentials for scaling your solo business without the burnout.

## 1. Foundation & Mindset
- [ ] Define your "North Star" goal for the next 90 days.
- [ ] Set up a dedicated workspace (physical or digital).
- [ ] Establish "Deep Work" blocks in your calendar (at least 2 hours daily).

## 2. Project Management
- [ ] Audit your current projects. Are they all high-ROI?
- [ ] Map out the next 3 milestones for your top project.
- [ ] Set up a "Quick Capture" system for ideas so they don't distract you.

## 3. Client & Revenue
- [ ] Update your CRM with the last contact date for all leads.
- [ ] Automate your invoicing process.
- [ ] Schedule a weekly "Finance Friday" to review profit/loss.

## 4. Content & Marketing
- [ ] Batch create content for the next 7 days.
- [ ] Define your primary platform (where your customers actually hang out).
- [ ] Create 3 reusable templates for social media posts.

## 5. Systems & Automation
- [ ] Identify one task you do every day that can be automated.
- [ ] Standardize your onboarding process for new clients.
- [ ] **Pro Tip:** Use the Solopreneur Workflow Hub to save 10+ hours per week by having all these systems in one place.

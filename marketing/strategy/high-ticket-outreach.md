# High-Ticket Outreach Strategy: $499 Workflow Audit

This strategy focuses on identifying and converting solopreneurs who have reached a "system ceiling"—where administrative friction is preventing further revenue growth.

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
*   **Persona**: Solo-Founders, Agency Owners (1-3 people), or High-Ticket Consultants.
*   **Industry**: Digital Services (Design, Development, Copywriting), Coaching/Consulting, Paid Ads Management.
*   **Business Stage**: Scaling from $100k to $500k ARR.
*   **Pain Points**:
    *   Spending 15+ hours a week on "Admin Drag" (Invoicing, lead sorting, manual follow-ups).
    *   Using 5+ disconnected tools (Slack, Trello, Google Sheets, PayPal, Calendly).
    *   "Search Time" fatigue: Spending 10 minutes looking for a file before starting work.
    *   Inconsistent client onboarding experience.
